About the Program

Chosen 2026 is the premier Jesus Youth Teens Ministry program designed specifically for teenagers to encounter the living Christ. Our mission is to help young hearts find their purpose and growth in a Christ-centered life through spiritual formation, fellowship, and joyful witness.

Now in its 4th successful year, Chosen continues to be a transformative journey for students across the region, building on the grace and testimony of the past three years.
